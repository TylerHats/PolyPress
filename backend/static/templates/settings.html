External SMTP Queue Limits & Setup Responsibility When using an external SMTP provider, PolyPress can only track connection errors and initial handoff issues. Once the email is accepted by your SMTP server, downstream delivery issues (e.g. recipient mailbox full or external rate-limiting blocks) are queued and managed on the provider's platform, and will not show up in the PolyPress outbox queue. Additionally, you are responsible for ensuring that all DNS configurations (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), email error handling, and bounce handlers are correctly configured directly at your external SMTP provider's server dashboard.
